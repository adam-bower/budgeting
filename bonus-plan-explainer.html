The Bonus Plan

A simple way to step out of the business — without selling it.

The Big Idea

We pay the team a 12% monthly bonus tied to the company's revenue — so they run the day-to-day, and I get my time back.

Why we're doing this

⏰

My time back

The team handles daily operations. I focus on finances, hiring, and family.

📈

Growth on autopilot

The team's bonus grows when revenue grows — they think like owners.

🛡️

Built-in protection

Slow months = smaller bonus. The cost flexes with the business.

How it works

For each team member, every month:

Their Bonus = 12% × Their Wage × Revenue Ratio

Revenue Ratio = this month's invoicing ÷ $70,000 (our normal month)

What if business slows down?

📉
Revenue drops Bonus shrinks automatically — the team feels it before we do.
🎯
Team responds They see slow months on the dashboard, find ways to bring in more work.
🛑
We can stop it The bonus is discretionary — we can pause or end it with 30 days notice.
